Welcome to our Placement page!
At NIT, we take pride in helping our students find great job opportunities after they graduate. Our placement cell works tirelessly to connect our students with top companies in various industries.
We believe that the quality of education we provide is reflected in the success of our students. Our graduates have gone on to work for leading companies in the country and around the world, and we are committed to maintaining this high standard of placement.
Our placement cell provides students with a range of services and resources, including:
Resume and cover letter writing assistance Interview preparation and practice sessions Career counseling and guidance Job and internship opportunities from leading companies Our placement cell also hosts a number of job fairs and recruiting events throughout the year, where students can meet recruiters and learn about potential job opportunities. We work with companies across various industries to provide our students with a wide range of opportunities.
At NIT, we understand that finding the right job can be a daunting task, especially in today's competitive job market. That's why we are committed to supporting our students every step of the way, from career counseling to job search and placement.
We are proud of our placement record, and we are committed to continuing to provide our students with the tools and resources they need to succeed in their careers. If you have any questions about our placement services, please don't hesitate to contact us. We look forward to helping you achieve your career goals!
